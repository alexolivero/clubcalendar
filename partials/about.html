About this site

View the schedules of all teams in My Teams. First, select a league from the left list, then select your team from the right. Remove a team by clicking on it in My Teams. To see more info about a match, click it within the calendar. Toggle the list of leagues and teams by clicking on the list header button.

Club Calendar was made with HTML, CSS, PHP, AngularJS, and Bootstrap. All data provided by api.football-data.org, thanks to Daniel Freitag. As soon as his data is updated, you'll see it here.

The goal of this website was to improve my API and web development skills and to get experience launching a website from scratch.

If this website is at all useful to you, that would make me really happy.
